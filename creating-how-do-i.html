Creating a How Do I?

A How Do I is a frequently asked question by your users that might help them immediately find a commonly requested piece of information. You will need to write 1) a question, 2) an answer, 3) have one or more URLs and the names for the pages ready (the URLs/links that answer the question or direct them to the form, etc.).

To create a new How Do I

  1. Go to the Workbench and click Create Content.

  2. Click How Do I

  3. Enter question (preferably starting with "How do I....", but not mandatory.

  4. Select the appropriate editorial section relevant to the question. If you are a pagemaster, it may automatically default to your page/section.

  5. Type in your answer, without the links or URLs to pages.

  6. Click on Links and enter each page name and then the URL (JEFF -- is this whole URL or a short URL /code-red?)

  7. Click Add another item for each URL. You only need one, but can have many.

  8. Click on Tagging and add the category or categories where this How Do I should appear. Can be multiple categories

  9. You can optionally add keywords to improve the ability to find the How Do I with a site search. For example, if the How Do I reads "How do I find information about pet licenses", you could enter cat license, dog license, registering dog, etc.

  10. Under Tagging, click on "Needs Review" to have your site editor review and publish the How Do I.
