Getting started

To create a new webform, go to My Workbench > Create Content.

You will see a standard content creation page. Enter a title, text to appear your form, and optional media files for the right sidebar or a PDF attachment version of the form.

On the next page you will see a list of field types on the left. You can drag the fields on to the From Preview pane on the left. Click on the pencil icon to change the field label and make the field required. There are a number of other options to explore on these tabs as well. Click the pencil icon again to close the form and drag another field onto the form preview. Click save when you are done.

Click the View tab to preview your form and make a test submission. Click Results to view your results and download a spreadsheet of all form submissions.

To set up email alerts, click on the Webform tab > Email Settings. You can enter an email address or choose a field value to send an email to. Manage other settings on the subsequent form

To change the text that appears after a form submission, or change the Submit button label text, click on the Advanced Settings tab.
